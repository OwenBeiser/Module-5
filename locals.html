@k — MK7 GTI

IN-229 - US-52 toward Brookville

Starting at OA on IN-229 in his MK7 GTI, keeping a smooth pace all the way down US-52 toward Brookville is k's way to go. It’s his go-to stretch for balanced corners and open straights — just enough twist to keep things exciting without ever feeling rushed. Is there a better way to spend a Sunday morning?

@grip - MK6 GLI

Hickory Road East than onto Pumphouse Road

Hickory Road East to Pumphouse Road is grip's favorite stretch to hit in his MK6 GLI. The sequence of S-turns on Hickory Road East is a highlight, offering a blend of technical driving and scenic views. Transitioning onto Pumphouse Road, the drive has a lot more hills with hidden curves that keep things interesting. He reccomends going at night for a better experience.

@The Driver — Fiesta ST 17'

IN-229 toward Ballstown

Starting next to Brums Woods on US-229 headed towards Ballstown is The Driver's go-to road in his Fiesta ST. This stretch offers a great mix of flowing curves and elevation changes, making it perfect for spirited driving. The Driver appreciates the balance of challenge and enjoyment this route provides, making it a favorite for weekend drives. Make sure to watch out for the crests!

@Zphil - Mustang GT 20'

Blue Creek Road into Brookville

Zphil's favorite stretch is Blue Creek Road into Brookville, especially in his Mustang GT. This route is a good blend of open straights, and good bends, making it a fun and engaging drive. The scenic views along Blue Creek Road add to the overall experience, making it a perfect choice for a leisurely yet spirited drive. Zphil reccomends watching out for deer though!
